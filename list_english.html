How to calculate a rental fee that neunode buyer get.

Computer rental income can be received from the fourth year of rental. This is because the compound interest will be used for the first three years after the loan. At the time of cancellation, the manufacturer purchases for the price of the number of computers, so compound interest increases the number of computers and maximizes the effect. As a general rule, the operating period at the time of contract is 5 years. Please contact us for operation after the 6th year. This simulation is the result of calculation when the price is always constant.

How to calculate tax

For the sake of convenience, the tax amount is calculated at 30% of general corporate tax. Individuals have different tax rates, so please contact us at the time of application for a detailed quote.
